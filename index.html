Coconut Grove · Florida

You don't have to be ready.
You just have to arrive.

Yoga in the Grove is a neighborhood studio on Virginia Street — a place to breathe, move, chant, and belong. We offer Hatha Asana, Kirtan, and community gatherings for anyone who finds their way through the door.

see the schedule
what we offer
i.

Hatha Asana

Traditional posture practice with an emphasis on breath, alignment, and steadiness — not performance. Classes are open to all levels; we meet you exactly where you are, every time.

ii.

Kirtan

Call-and-response chanting that opens the heart and quiets the mind. No musical experience necessary — just a willingness to let your voice be heard, however it sounds.

iii.

Seva

The yoga of selfless service — showing up for each other and for our neighborhood. This is how the practice extends beyond the mat and into the life of the Grove.

Come for the practice.
Stay for the people.

This is not a fitness studio with a loyalty program. It's a gathering place — a living room, a porch, a circle of regulars who leave the door open for anyone new. We've had beginners and longtime practitioners sit side by side for years, and that's exactly how we like it. Whatever brought you here, you belong in the room.
